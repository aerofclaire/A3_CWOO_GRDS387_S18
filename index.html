Food photo  journaling app for everyone

One of the keys to successful dieting and weight loss is to maintain the consistency in what you eat! However, isn't it tough to remember, search and count the calories for every meal?

Panel, is a simple app that allows you to take a photo before every meal!

Record what you eat via photogrphy in a simple click!

Download Panel Learn more

What is so special about Panel?

Features useful for efficiently managing your meals, calculating your calories and controlling your weight!

Sync in your calendar

Record your photos in calendar view with location and customisable tags and notes!

Camera or camera roll

Quick access to take photos within the app or choose to upload photos from your camera roll!

Calorie Recording

Calculate and record your calories so you can keep track of the amount you can consume during the day!

Start snapping today to efficiently maintain your weight goals & healthy diet!
